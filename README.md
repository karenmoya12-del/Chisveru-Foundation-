# Chisveru-Foundation-

### Dignity. Empowerment. Change.

Official website for **Chisveru Foundation** — a Zimbabwe-based NGO dedicated to ending period poverty through handmade, washable, reusable sanitary pads and menstrual health education.

-----

## 🌸 About

Chisveru Foundation creates and distributes reusable sanitary pads to girls and women who need them most. Every pad is handmade with care, designed to last up to 2 years, and distributed free of charge — keeping girls in school and women in their communities.

**Our pillars:**

- 🎓 Keep girls in school
- 💪 Empower women
- 🌿 Protect the planet
- 📚 Educate communities

## 🖥️ About This Site

This is the static website for Chisveru Foundation, built with plain HTML and CSS — no frameworks, no dependencies. Fast, lightweight, and fully responsive.

**Features:**

- Full-width logo hero banner
- Mission & pillars section
- How It Works process steps
- Real product photography
- Behind the Pads media gallery (photos + video)
- Get Involved / ways to support
- Community testimonials
- Donation CTA
- Contact form
- Mobile responsive
