Our Mission

Period Poverty Ends Here

Across many communities, girls miss school and women miss work every month due to lack of affordable sanitary products. Chisveru Foundation believes menstrual hygiene is not a privilege — it is a right.

We hand-make high-quality washable, reusable sanitary pads and distribute them to girls and women who need them most, alongside education on menstrual health and hygiene.

🎓

Keep Girls in School

No girl should miss class due to her period.

💪

Empower Women

Full participation in economic and social life.

🌿

Protect the Planet

Reusable pads reduce waste and pollution.

📚

Educate Communities

Breaking stigma through open conversation.

The Process

From Our Hands to Her Future

Every pad is lovingly made and carefully delivered to the girls and women who need it most.

1

Source Materials

We source safe, absorbent, skin-friendly fabrics from ethical suppliers to craft quality pads.

2

Handcraft Pads

Skilled volunteers and community women sew each pad with care, creating local employment too.

3

Educate & Train

We run menstrual health education sessions so recipients know how to use and care for their pads.

4

Distribute

Pads are distributed directly to girls in schools and women in communities, free of charge.

Our Product

Pads Made With Love & Purpose

Our washable reusable sanitary pads are designed for comfort, reliability, and longevity — crafted to give women the confidence to go about their day, every day.

🛡️

Leak-Proof Protection

Multi-layer design with a waterproof inner lining keeps every girl protected and confident.

🌸

Skin-Safe Fabrics

Made with soft, breathable cotton that is gentle on skin and free of harmful chemicals.

♻️

Up to 2 Years of Use

Each pad can be washed and reused for up to 2 years, replacing up to 200 disposables.

🤝

Made by Community Women

Our production process creates skills and income for women in local communities.
